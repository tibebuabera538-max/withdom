Company History

1. The Era of Foundation (1894 - 1930s)

The history of Ethiopian telecommunications began in 1894 during the reign of Emperor Menelik II. The first 477 km telegraph line was stretched between Harar and Addis Ababa. Although the public initially viewed the technology with suspicion, the King expanded it, believing communication was vital for national growth.

2. Structural Change and Growth

After the Italian occupation, the institution was reorganized in 1953. The start of satellite communication in the 1960s connected Ethiopia to the world. Connectivity increased as microwave radio technologies were put into operation.

3. Modern Ethio Telecom Chapter

In 2011, the company changed its name to "Ethio Telecom" and carried out massive reform works. By introducing 4G LTE and 5G networks, as well as launching the telebirr digital payment system, it is leading the country toward digital prosperity.
